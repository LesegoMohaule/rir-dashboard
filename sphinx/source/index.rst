RIR Platform
==============================================

Welcome to the Risk Informed Response (RIR) Platform!

.. image:: img/rir-platform.png
   :width: 703px
   :height: 324px
   :scale: 100 %
   :alt: alternate text
   :align: center

The RIR Platform is a tool for humanitarian workers to plan for and 
mitigate against risk. The platform is Open Source and can be
shared and reused for your own purposes under the terms of our license.
